Welcome to my CFG project!

I am currently a student about to enter my fourth year of an English Literature/History of Art MA at Glasgow University.

I am also the current creative director of the Glasgow University Charity Fashion Show (GUCFS) for 2020/21. I worked on committee as fashion coordinater for 'IN BLOOM' - the GUCFS 2020 campaign where I obtained collections and styled them for the shoots and final show.

I have created this website as an online, visual extension of the styling work detailed in my CV as part of an online coding course I have undertaken with Code First Girls.
