IN BLOOM SHOW

As part of the Glasgow University Charity Fashion Show (GUCFS) 2020 campaign, I obtained and styled half of the collections featuring in the show. I worked with the following designers:

  • Nadia Pinkney
  • Orlanda James
  • Greg Brears

  • Holly Cunningham

INCLINE

I also styled our editorial campiagns which lead up to our final show in February 2020. IN BLOOM aimed to showcase strength in adversity, this shoot exploring the athletic, traditional notions of strength. I styled the menswear for this shoot, working with Scottish design graduate, Shona Hamilton.

INHABIT

For this shoot, we wanted to turn traditional notions of masculinity on their head with our 'blush boy' models. I collaborated with Zexi Yu here who's carefully embroidered, gender fluid collection worked beautifully against the backdrop of the Glasgow City Chambers.

INVERT

This shoot was intended to be the opposite of INHABIT. Here we wanted to show glamorous, female-aligned models in a space traditioanlly dominated by men: the office space. Poppy Camden was the designer for this shoot - her collection of altered and exaggerated tailored pieces working well with the concept.
