UNITY SUMMER 2019

I wrote my first piece for the Glasgow University Magazine (GUM) on the evolution of subcultures. This piece was for the style section and was published in the summer 2019 issue; 'Unity'. I was able to draw on my interest in trends and the sociology behind them in order to write this article.

HYPE WEEK

During refreshers week in January 2020 or 'Hype Week', I wrote a science article for GUM's online platform about eco-anxiety. I enjoyed writing my first science article as it allowed me to explore and express my strong, personal views about the climate crisis.

SOMETHING IN THE WATER

For the Spring 2020 issue; 'Something in the Water', I wrote another science piece. This one was focused on the psychology behind cult behavior and their leaders. I found writing this piece very rewarding as it required a lot of personal research and taught me a great deal.

BLOSSOM ZINE

Blossom Zine was a one-off, student-run publication created to raise money for Glasgow Women's Aid. The zine primarily explored femininity and gender-based violence so my piece was centred around traditional attitudes to gender-based violence.
